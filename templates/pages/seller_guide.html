Seller Guide

Everything you need to start selling on QuickDealr — from listing to payout.

01

Register as a Seller

During registration, select the Seller role. Your account is reviewed and activated. Once active, you can access the Seller Dashboard from the navigation bar.

02

Add a Product

Click Add Product in the navbar. Fill in the product name, description, price, category, and stock. Upload a clear product image. For auctions, toggle the auction option and select a duration using the quick-select buttons (DD:HH:MM format).

03

Wait for Admin Approval

All listings are reviewed by the QuickDealr admin team for quality and compliance. You will receive a notification once your product is approved or rejected. Approved products go live immediately.

04

Manage Orders

When a buyer places an order, you will see it in the Orders section of your dashboard. Update the order status as you process and ship it. Buyers are notified at each status change.

05

Withdraw Earnings

Your earnings accumulate in your seller wallet after each completed order. Submit a withdrawal request from the Withdraw page. Withdrawals are processed by the admin team within 2–3 business days.
