Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites work correctly and provide information to operators.

Cookies We Use

QuickDealr uses only essential session cookies for authentication and security. These are deleted when you close your browser or log out. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Third-Party Cookies

We use Google Fonts for typography which may set its own cookies. We do not use advertising networks or social media tracking pixels.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from staying logged in.

Policy Updates

If we change our cookie use, this page will be updated with the new information and a revised date.
