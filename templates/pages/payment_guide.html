Payment Guide

Learn about the payment methods available on QuickDealr and how each one works.

W

Wallet Payment

Recommended. Top up your QuickDealr wallet and use it for instant, fee-free payments across the platform. Wallet balance is credited within 24 hours of a verified top-up request.

  • Instant checkout — no card details needed
  • Secure and admin-verified
  • Works for both regular orders and auction wins
C

Cash on Delivery (COD)

Pay in cash when your order arrives at your doorstep. Available for most standard product orders. Not available for auction wins.

  • No advance payment required
  • Pay only when you receive the item
  • Available on eligible orders
K

Card Payment

Pay securely using your debit or credit card at checkout. Card details are validated server-side and protected by CSRF security. We do not store raw card numbers.

  • Visa, Mastercard, and RuPay accepted
  • Secure, encrypted checkout form
  • Instant confirmation
U

UPI / QR Code

Scan a QR code or use any UPI app to complete payment. Fast and familiar for Indian users. Payment is verified before the order is confirmed.

  • Works with any UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Instant digital payment
  • No hidden fees
