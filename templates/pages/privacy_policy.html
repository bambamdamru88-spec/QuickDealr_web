Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide during registration (name, email, phone), data generated through platform use (bid history, orders, wallet transactions), and technical data such as IP addresses for security and fraud prevention.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to operate the platform, process transactions, send notifications, detect fraud, and improve our services. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on secured servers. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. Payment data is validated server-side and not stored in raw form. All forms are CSRF-protected.

Cookies

We use only session cookies required for authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Disabling cookies will prevent you from logging in.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting support@quickdealr.com. We respond within 30 days of a verified request.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
