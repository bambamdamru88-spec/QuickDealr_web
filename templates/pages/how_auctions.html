How Auctions Work

Everything you need to know about live bidding on QuickDealr.

01

Seller Lists a Product

A verified seller creates an auction listing with a starting price and a duration (from 1 hour up to 1 week). Once approved by an admin, the auction goes live and is visible to all buyers.

02

Buyers Watch and Bid

Any registered buyer can enter a live auction page. Bids must exceed the current highest bid. The platform uses real-time WebSocket technology — every new bid is instantly shown to all watchers without refreshing the page.

03

Outbid Notifications

If someone outbids you, you receive an immediate in-app notification. You can return to the auction page and place a higher bid before the timer runs out.

04

Auction Ends — Winner Declared

When the countdown reaches zero, the highest bidder wins the item. Both the winner and the seller are notified. The winning bid amount is the final purchase price.

05

Payment and Delivery

The winner completes payment via Wallet, Card, UPI, or COD within the payment window. The seller then ships the item and updates the order status. You can download your invoice at any time.
