Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Standard Orders

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original condition. Approved refunds are credited to your wallet within 5 business days.

Auction Wins

Auction wins are generally non-refundable once payment is completed. Refunds may be considered only if the item significantly differs from the listing.

Order Cancellations

Orders cancelled before shipping are refunded in full to your wallet. Post-shipment cancellations are handled case-by-case.

Wallet Top-Ups

Approved wallet top-up amounts are non-refundable to the original payment source. Wallet balance can be used for future purchases.

How to Request

Contact support@quickdealr.com with your order number and reason. We aim to resolve all requests within 5 business days.
